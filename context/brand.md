# Éclosens — Brand Context
<!-- Updated 2026-05-21 — Color palette aligned with logo (blue + yellow) -->

## Brand Name
Éclosens

## Primary Tagline
Accompagnement FIV et PMA au Luxembourg

## Secondary Tagline
Expertise FIV, soutien humain et accompagnement émotionnel du parcours de fertilité.

---

# Brand Positioning

Éclosens accompanies women and couples facing infertility, IVF/FIV and PMA journeys through a deeply human, emotionally supportive and expert approach.

The brand combines:
- real IVF expertise (26 years in AMP/FIV environments)
- emotional accompaniment
- calm reassurance
- trust
- human understanding
- emotional safety

Shiatsu is presented as a complementary support approach, not as the core business positioning.

The main positioning is:
EXPERT IVF/PMA ACCOMPANIMENT.

---

# Core Differentiator

The strongest differentiator of Éclosens is:
26 years of experience inside IVF/AMP environments.

Key elements:
- CMCO Schiltigheim
- IVF laboratory management in Switzerland
- real understanding of medical and emotional realities
- long-term field experience

# Color Palette (Official)

These colors are derived from the Éclosens logo and must be used consistently:

Main background:       #F8F6F2  (warm white)
Primary text:          #2F2F2F
Brand blue (primary):  #8CC6E8  (logo background color — main identity color)
Soft blue background:  #DCEEF8
Warm beige:            #E7D8C8
Warm light bg:         #F3ECE4
Borders:               #E8E2DA
Yellow accent:         #F2E29B  (logo warm circle — use subtly)

Button primary: bg #8CC6E8, text #1A5C80 (dark navy)
Interactive blue (hover): #6DB5E0

The blue is the MAIN identity color.
The yellow is a SUBTLE warm accent only.
