# Create Blog Article Workflow

## Objective
Generate an SEO-focused blog article for the Éclosens fertility accompaniment website.

## Inputs
- target keyword
- article subject
- emotional angle
- SEO intent

## Instructions
1. Write emotionally intelligent content
2. Use calm and reassuring tone
3. Structure content with semantic headings
4. Include introduction, body and FAQ
5. Add internal links to pillar pages
6. Optimize for SEO naturally
7. Avoid keyword stuffing
8. Avoid medical claims

## Output
- long-form article
- SEO title
- meta description
- FAQ section

## Edge Cases
- Medical misinformation
- Repetitive topics
- Overlapping blog content
